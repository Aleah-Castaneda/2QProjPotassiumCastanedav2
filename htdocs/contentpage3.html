Puerto Princesa Underground River

Journey into Puerto Princesa's famed Underground River, one of Palawan's most popular tourist destinations and a world-renowned natural wonder. It features jaw-dropping cave formations, stunning limestone cliffs, and crystal-clear waters that will surely make the most of your travel experience. Visit this beautiful tropical paradise to see firsthand why it's a true wonder of the world.

Activities

Sabang X-Zipline

Seeing Mayon Volcano in person is amazing, but seeing it up close is much more so! If you’re up for an adventure, you can hike and trek the Mayon Volcano to see it closely. You may choose between two trails: the Buyuhan trail and the Buang trail. Also, keep in mind that the walk might take up to two days. However, you should first contact the Department of Tourism in Legazpi City before hiking Mayon Volcano.

Monkey Trail

You may return to Sabang on foot after your subterranean river trip by walking the 5-kilometer Monkey Trail. A steep trek through overgrown woodland and karst limestones leads to this off-the-beaten-path destination. While this journey will take longer than the typical boat voyage back to the starting point, it is far more thrilling, especially for hikers and adventure-seekers. It's a longer and more picturesque trip.

Ugong Rock Adventures

The sound that echoes when you smash or pound on the walls is referred to as "ugong." This natural wonder reaches a height of 75 feet. Spelunking is a great option for extreme adventurers who wish to have a closer look at the formation. Because you'll be squeezing through small caves, apertures, and slipping into holes, this is not for the faint of heart. The 360-degree perspective of the surroundings is further highlighted by these exciting activities.

To get the most out of your vacation, keep these vital travel recommendations in mind:

  • Wear light, comfortable clothing that can get wet and allow you to move around, and bring a change of clothes as well.
  • Bring a dry bag with you to protect your belongings.
  • Before the tour, apply sunscreen and mosquito repellent.
  • Wear rubber slippers, hiking sandals, or aqua shoes.
  • Wear the provided life vests and hard caps at all times for your own safety.
  • Refillable bottles should be used.
  • To prevent disturbing the bats, it is forbidden to speak loudly inside the cave chambers.
